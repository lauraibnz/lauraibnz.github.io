Laura Ibáñez-Martínez is a PhD student and researcher at the Music Technology Group (MTG), Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF). Her work focuses on controllable music generation from disentangled representations, and her thesis is being carried out under the supervision of Xavier Serra and Martin Rocamora.
